At some point, math and writing became cooler than music to me. My musician dreams felt hedonistic and corrupt. Every morning I would wake up and groan over my reality. It was unbearable how pointless my life had become. The illusion I'd steeped myself in was gone. I felt ugly and naked. And the world of logic and abstraction became a doorway.
